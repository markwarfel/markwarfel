Cookie Policy

  1. 1. What Are Cookies?

    Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences and enhance your user experience.

  2. 2. How We Use Cookies

    We use cookies to improve the functionality of our platform, remember your preferences, and analyze how users interact with our website. Some cookies are essential for basic operations, while others are used for analytics.

  3. 3. Types of Cookies We Use

    Essential Cookies: These are necessary for the website to function properly, such as allowing you to log into your account.

    Analytics Cookies: We use these cookies to track usage and performance to improve the platform.

    Preference Cookies: These store your preferences, such as language settings or saved lists.

  4. 4. Managing Cookies

    You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling cookies may impact the functionality of certain features on the platform.

  5. 5. Changes to Cookie Policy

    We may update our Cookie Policy periodically. Any changes will be posted on this page.
